#SnapNotSell

Because Snapchat should be for connection — not constant ads.

Why We’re Speaking Out

Snapchat has become overloaded with ads, interrupting the simple, fun experience it once offered. We’re not against ads — but we are against the overwhelming number of them, especially when they ruin the flow of stories and conversations. It’s time to speak up.

What We’re Asking For

  • ✅ Fewer ad interruptions
  • ✅ Better user control over ad preferences
  • ✅ A return to the Snapchat we loved

How You Can Help

Join the movement on social media. Tweet, post, and share your experience using #SnapNotSell. Tag @Snapchat and tell them how the ad overload is affecting you. Together, we can make them listen.
